Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need a sprinkler system if it rains so much?

Even with the rain, you may notice that your lawn still isn't as green and healthy as you'd like. It takes consistent and even water coverage to get perfect grass. And remember, the last thing you want to count on is the Alaskan weather.

How long does a sprinkler system take to install?

Our typical residential installs take about 3 days. Larger homes can sometimes take 4-5 days.

How much does a system typically cost?

Every lawn and system is so different that we don't give any numbers until we see your property. Quotes are free!

Do I need to be home for the guys to come do a repair?

If you have an outdoor controller we don't even need to enter your house! If your controller is inside we are happy to make arrangments with you.
